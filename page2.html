Learn Amharic

There are several languages spoken in Ethiopia, and they are each wrapped inside of culture and tradition. While this site can’t teach you to speak perfect Amharic like an Ethiopian, it does aim to give you introductory words and phrases you can use when speaking to Ethiopians in their national language, Amharic.

Essential Words & Phrases

Common Amharic phrases with pronunciation and English translation
Amharic Pronunciation English
ሰላም ነው? Selam new? Hello (lit. is it peace?)
እንዴት ነህ/ነሽ? Endet neh/nesh? How are you?
ደህና ነኝ Dehna negn I am fine
እግዚአብሔር ይመስገን Egziabher ymesgen Let God be praised
ስሜ … እባላለሁ Sime … ebalalehu My name is …
ስምህ/ስምሽ ማነው? Simih/simish manew? What is your name?
ቤተሰብ እንዴት ነው? Beteseb endet new? How is your family?
አማርኛ Amargna Amharic

The key to learning any new language is practicing every day. Amharic is no different. If you truly want to learn the language, to master it, start by learning the alphabet. Amharic uses the Ge’ez alphabet, a language which is no longer spoken (outside of occasional use in religious settings in Ethiopia). Click here for a reference to the Amharic fidel (letters).
